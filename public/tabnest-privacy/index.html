Privacy Policy

Tab Nest

Provided by Off By 1 LLC  ·  Effective June 9, 2026  ·  Last updated June 12, 2026

The short version

Tab Nest stores your tab and session data in a private application-data area of your own Google Drive that only Tab Nest can reach — it never appears among your normal Drive files. We don't collect it, we never see it, and it isn't sent to any server we run. There are no analytics, no advertising, and no selling or sharing of your data — there's nothing to sell.

01 — Who this covers

The basics

This policy explains how the Tab Nest browser extension ("Tab Nest," "the extension") handles information. Tab Nest is published by Off By 1 LLC, a Washington limited liability company ("we," "us," "our"). It applies to your use of the extension and to the Google account data the extension accesses on your behalf.

02 — What Tab Nest does

Saving and syncing your tabs

Tab Nest lets you save, organize, and restore browser tabs and tab groups. To make your saved tabs available across your devices, Tab Nest can sync that data to your own Google Drive using Google's authorization (OAuth). Drive sync is something you turn on by signing in with your Google account; if you don't connect Drive, your data stays only in your browser.

03 — Information Tab Nest handles

Your tab data, and your Google Drive

The information Tab Nest works with is the content you choose to save in it — for example, the page titles and URLs of tabs you store, the names of your tab groups, and your extension settings.

When you enable Drive sync, Tab Nest uses the drive.appdata authorization scope. This scope lets Tab Nest read and write only to a hidden Application Data folder that Google sets aside for it in your Drive. Files saved there are not visible in your normal Drive view, and Tab Nest cannot see, open, or touch any of your other Drive files. The data is stored within your own Drive storage and remains under your control.

Tab Nest does not request your name, email address, contacts, browsing history, or any other personal information beyond what's described above.

04 — What we collect

Nothing reaches us

Off By 1 LLC operates no server or backend for Tab Nest. Your saved data moves directly between your browser and your own Google Drive. We do not receive, store, log, or have access to your tab data, your Drive contents, or your Google account credentials. We do not use cookies, analytics SDKs, fingerprinting, or any third-party tracking inside the extension.

05 — How Google user data is used

Limited Use commitment

Tab Nest's access to information from Google APIs is limited to providing and improving the tab-sync features described above. Tab Nest's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. Specifically, Tab Nest does not:

  • transfer or sell Google user data to third parties, ad platforms, data brokers, or for any advertising purpose;
  • use Google user data for serving ads;
  • allow humans to read your Google user data, except where you give explicit consent for specific files, where it's necessary for security or to comply with applicable law, or where the data has been aggregated and anonymized.

06 — Sharing

We don't share what we don't have

Because we don't collect your data, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose. The only party your data is exchanged with is Google, as the provider of the Drive storage you've chosen to sync to, under Google's own Privacy Policy.

07 — Keeping and deleting your data

You're in control

Your Tab Nest data persists in your browser and, if sync is enabled, in your Google Drive for as long as you keep it. To remove it:

  • Delete saved tabs or clear data from within the extension.
  • Remove Tab Nest's hidden app data from Drive: open Drive settings → Manage apps → Tab Nest → Options → Delete hidden app data.
  • Revoke Tab Nest's access to your Google account at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Uninstall the extension to remove its locally stored data.

08 — Security

How your data is protected

Data synced to Google Drive is protected by Google's account security and encryption. Data stored locally is protected by your browser and device. Because no copy of your data reaches a server we operate, there is no central store on our side to be breached.

09 — Children

Not directed to children

Tab Nest is a general-audience productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
